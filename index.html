About Me

Hi, I'm Angel Salviejo. I live in Southville 3, Poblacion, Muntinlupa City. I'm 22 years old, just celebrated my birthday on July 11. I have three siblings and I am the youngest. I live with my parents, as my siblings are all married. My father works as a family driver in Ayala, while my mother has been working abroad for over 9 years. It's just me and my father living in our house, and I take care of everything. I am also the only one being supported in my studies, and my parents expect me to finish my education. I wanted to take a course in Criminology, but I didn't have a choice because I was placed in this course by PLMUN. My expectation for this subject is to learn a lot and not to feel pressured. Five years from now, I see myself having passed the civil service exam and working in the Bureau of Corrections to make my parents prouds and be the reason to back their sacrifices.
